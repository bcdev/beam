Development has been concluded

Please note that BEAM is not further developed. It will remain under limited maintenance. During this maintenance phase we continue to support you when facing problems. However, we encourage all BEAM users to use SNAP from now on. SNAP is the successor and evolution of BEAM. When installing SNAP along with the Sentinel-3 Toolbox you will have the same great user experience as with BEAM. You can even extend the number of features by installing other toolboxes like those for Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2, CHRIS-Box SMOS-Box and Proba-V Toolbox.
Thanks to all our dear and loyal BEAM users. We hope that you turn into SNAP users now.

BEAM has been developed by Brockmann Consult GmbH under contract to ESA.
